Bio

Dr. Yan Yan is currently a tenured Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Illinois Chicago. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Trento. He has published 200+ research papers in the fields of computer vision, multimedia and machine learning. He received IBM Best Student Paper Award in ICPR 2014, Best Paper Award in ACM Multimedia 2015 and Best Paper Finalist in ACM Multimedia 2018. He has served as the General Chair for ACM ICMR 2025 and as an Area Chair for major computer vision and multimedia conferences including CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, BMVC, ACM Multimedia ICLR and NeurIPS. He also served as associate editors in Neurocomputing, Computer Vision and Image Understanding (CVIU), Machine Vision and Applications (MVA), Image and Vision Computing (IVC), and guest editors in IEEE Transactions on Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence (TPAMI), Computer Vision and Image Understanding (CVIU) and ACM Transactions on Multimedia Computing, Communications, and Applications (TOMM). Know more information please check the CVM-LAB.
